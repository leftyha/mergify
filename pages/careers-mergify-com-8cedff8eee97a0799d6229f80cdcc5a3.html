Working at Mergify

You’ve made it here—awesome! That means you’re curious about joining the team behind Mergify. Let’s tell you why that’s a great idea.

At Mergify, we’re on a mission to remove the frustration from CI/CD. We’re building the default companion for every CI system—helping developers merge code faster, with more confidence, and zero noise.

We’re a remote-first, product-focused company driven by our values: collaboration, openness, ownership, and efficiency. We believe in autonomy, asynchronous work, and building a culture where everyone’s ideas matter—regardless of title or timezone.

We’re bootstrapped, customer-funded, and ambitious. We move fast, iterate constantly, and learn every day. If you’re passionate about building great tools for other developers—and want to help shape the future of how software is shipped—we’d love to meet you.
